Not An Octopus helps you create truly unique memories and family treasures.

Colour In Theatre

Custom-designed cardboard puppet theatre for kids to colour in. Then let them put on their own puppet shows! Theatre theme and graphics can be designed with your child!

Stamp Kit

This kit has everything you and your child need to design and cut your own stamps. You can choose the kit’s theme and even provide templates (photos, your child’s favourite toys...). Makes a great activity for friends to do together – and trade stamps when they’re done!

Monster Game

Give “Pin the Tail on the Donkey” a new spin or create an entirely new favourite party game! We’ll consult with the birthday child to come up with a game that will be just what he or she wanted.

Colour-in Canvas

The guests will have fun, and your child will really feel like the star of the party. Show off the finished paintings afterward to let the little artists take pride in their work!

Jumpy Castle

super-heroes.
