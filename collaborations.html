Artist Collaborations

We’re proud of our international artist collaborations to offer you a wide variation of designs. Our very first artist in residency is Manuela Büchting from Germany. Once you’re ready to fill out the order form, you can decide whether your final product(s) will be designed by us or Manu. Here’s a bit more info on her and her amazing work:

MANUELA BÜCHTING

Manuela Büchting (manubuechting.com) is a documentarian, media educator, illustrator and graphic artist from the Harz mountains. Studied Fine Arts in Braunschweig, Germany, and finished an MFA in Documentary Media in Toronto, ON, Canada.

She worked in Literary Arts at The Banff Centre, for the Toronto International Film Festival and with bookbinders and media artists.
