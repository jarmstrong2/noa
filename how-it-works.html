How it Works

  • 1. Fill out the form below. We will respond within 5 business days. We will email you to share ideas, samples and prices. We might also schedule an in-person meeting (GTA only) or Skype session with you and your child to develop ideas and goods together. A fully refundable down payment is required at this stage.

  • 2. Together we will set a time frame for your products to be developed, after which samples are going to be sent to you for your review. Up to this point, you can cancel your order at any time. If you choose not to proceed at this point, your down payment will be fully refunded.

  • 3. We will complete your order punctually by a mutually agreed deadline and deliver your goods to a location of your choosing – your house, workplace or party location.

We are excited to create unique arts and craft with you and your kids!
