Handmade Calendar

A perfect gift for your relatives (or yourself): Hand-made calendar featuring your child and hand-felted details (designed with your personal style in mind).

Photo by the amazing Kimberly Walker (www.kimberlywalker.ca)

Custom Book

Children 6 months and up can develop a story with us; we can provide the illustrations or help your child to illustrate it. The book is professionally bound and will be a treasure to keep forever.

Holiday Crafts

Make your holiday celebrations memorable with customized holiday crafts, designed with or for your child!

Custom Comic

Let your child become a superhero! We’ll develop the story with him/her and create drawings suitable for colouring in. Your child will be author, illustrator and super-hero all at once!

Perpetual Magnetic Calendar

This metal plate calendar will never lose its charm – or its usefulness. We’ll help you find a theme that speaks to you, feature your child(ren) on it, hand-paint it, and adorn it with hand-made photo magnets. You can add new magnets each month!

Custom Action Figure

Children are super-heroes – they deserve their very own action figure! Custom designed and hand-painted.

Modern Art

Make your child’s favourite toy a grown-up piece of abstract art. A photo of your child’s beloved plush is digitally reduced to one pixel, a single block of colour, encapsulating your child’s passions, personality and memories.
